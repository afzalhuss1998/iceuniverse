🏏 Cricket Highlights

IPL 2025 Auction Shatters Records

The IPL 2025 auction concluded with record-breaking bids that have redefined player valuations in T20 cricket. Australian pace bowler Mitchell Starc became the most expensive player ever at ₹24.75 crore, while several young talents received life-changing contracts.
